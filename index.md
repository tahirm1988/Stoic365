# Privacy Policy for Stoic365

**Effective date:** May 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **MTdevelopment 2025** ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the **Stoic365** mobile application ("App").

Stoic365 is a daily self-improvement and reflection app based on Stoic principles. We are committed to being transparent about how the App works and how user data is handled.

## 1. Information We Collect

Stoic365 is designed to work primarily on your device. We do not require you to create an account.

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

- Your onboarding preferences, such as selected categories
- Your app settings, such as whether notifications are enabled
- Your progress, such as completed days and related timestamps
- Your reflection entries that you write inside the App
- Your premium access status, such as whether Stoic365 Pro has been unlocked

This information is stored locally on your device using on-device storage.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect Through Our Own Servers

We do not collect, transmit, or store your reflection entries, progress data, or settings on our own servers.

At this time, Stoic365 does not provide account registration, cloud sync, or a developer-operated backend for user data.

## 3. Notifications

Stoic365 can send local reminders, such as morning and evening reminders, if you choose to enable notifications. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device.

If notifications are disabled by you or denied at the operating system level, the App will not send reminder notifications.

## 4. Purchases

Stoic365 may offer premium content or features through in-app purchases, such as a one-time Pro unlock. Purchases are processed by the Google Play Store or other platform provider, not directly by us.

We do not receive your full payment card details. We may only receive purchase status information needed to unlock premium functionality in the App.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on platform or library functionality necessary for core features, such as:

- Google Play Billing for in-app purchases
- Operating system notification services for local reminders

These services may process limited technical information as required for their operation under their own policies.

## 6. How We Use Information

Any information stored by the App is used only to provide the App’s functionality, including:

- Showing your daily progress
- Saving your reflections
- Remembering your preferences
- Restoring premium access status where applicable
- Scheduling reminders if you enable them

## 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your reflection entries, progress, or settings with third parties through our own servers.

However, certain platform providers, such as Google Play, may process limited information when handling in-app purchases or platform functionality, subject to their own privacy policies.

## 8. Data Retention

Because Stoic365 stores most data locally on your device, your data generally remains on your device until:

- You delete it
- You clear the App’s storage through your device settings
- You uninstall the App

We do not maintain a separate developer-hosted user database for this App at this time.

## 9. Data Deletion

You can delete locally stored App data by:

- Uninstalling the App from your device, or
- Clearing the App’s storage/data in your device settings

Since Stoic365 does not currently require user accounts, there is no account-based deletion workflow at this time.

## 10. Children’s Privacy

Stoic365 is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through a developer-operated backend.

## 11. Security

We take reasonable steps to design the App in a privacy-conscious way, including minimizing data collection and storing core user content locally on the device whenever possible. However, no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 12. International Users

If you use the App from outside **Bosnia and Herzegovina**, you understand that applicable platform providers may process certain technical or transaction-related data according to their own systems and policies.

## 13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.

Your continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.
